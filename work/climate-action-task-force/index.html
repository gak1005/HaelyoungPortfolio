OVERVIEW

Climate Action Task Force (CATF) is an environmental organization at Emily Carr University that seeks to inform student designers and artists of the on-going fight against climate change and the ways we as a collective can take part.

We decided to focus on the conflict of destructive palm oil plantations in Southeast Asia and create ways for our target audience to gain motivation to learn more on this environmental issue and also take part in minimizing the damage that is being putting upon Earth.

4 KEYWORDS

artificial, chaotic, vintage & evocative

APPROACH

We emphasized artificiality and chaos with usage of colours and photos in our branding so as to reflect what's happening to our planet. On top of that idea, we aimed to evoke curiosity from our target audience by taking inspiration from vintage comic books. That way, we can create a more light-hearted and easier means for the audience to interact with our designs and the topic of palm oil plantation, and ultimately it will inform wider array of people on this issue.

public theatre
poster series

OVERVIEW

Public Theatre is a world renowned venue for performances and plays. This project aims to create a visual system for their upcoming season featuring Shakespearean plays like Othello, Hamlet and As you Like it.

APPROACH

Bold lines and vibrant colour palette of magenta and yellow are used in inspiration from pop art, as I saw this art genre in alignment to the bustling lifestyle of New York in which the venue is located.

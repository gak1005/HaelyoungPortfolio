urban source
brand identity

2020 / BRAND IDENTITY

OVERVIEW

Urban Source is an art alternative store that sells manufacture discards from local businesses. Despite being a small store, Urban Source has thrived for over two decades due to its special charms that big corporations typically don't have: a sense of warmness and their genuine support for sustainability. This project's objective is to create a distinctive brand identity for Urban Source which they're currently lacking, so that they can continue to grow as an ecofriendly business.

4 KEYWORDS

friendly, earthy, energetic & artistic

APPROACH

The main focus amongst the keywords will be on artistic, which I find it to be synonymous to inspirational. With that, I aimed to brand Urban Source to be more than just a store that sells manufacture discards, but a place that breathes ideas into its visitors with the unusual objects that they sell.

The design elements in this project too, are inspired from the materials that are available at Urban Source, meaning the components can be created by the store staffs themselves with the inventory that is already accessible to them.

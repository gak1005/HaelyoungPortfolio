OVERVIEW<\/span><\/p>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t

Climate Action Task Force (CATF) is an environmental organization at Emily Carr University that\nseeks to inform student designers and artists of the on-going fight against\nclimate change and the ways we as a collective can take part. <\/span>\n<\/p>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t

\nWe decided to focus on the conflict of destructive palm oil plantations in Southeast Asia and create ways for our target audience to gain motivation to learn more on this environmental issue and also take part in minimizing the damage that is being putting upon Earth. \n<\/span><\/p>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\nWe emphasized artificiality and chaos with usage of colours and photos in our branding so as to reflect what's happening to our planet. On top of that idea, we aimed to evoke curiosity from our target audience by taking inspiration from vintage comic books.

OVERVIEW<\/span><\/p>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t

Urban Source is an art alternative store that sells manufacture discards from local businesses. Despite being a small store, Urban Source has thrived for over two decades due to its special charms that big corporations typically don't have: a sense of warmness and their genuine support for sustainability. This project's objective is to create a distinctive brand identity for Urban Source which they're currently lacking, so that they can continue to grow as an ecofriendly business. <\/span><\/p>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

APPROACH<\/span><\/p>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t

The main focus amongst the keywords will be on artistic, which I find it to be synonymous to inspirational. With that, I aimed to brand Urban Source to be more than just a store that sells manufacture discards, but a place that breathes ideas into its visitors with the unusual objects that they sell.<\/span><\/p>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
